                  Primary School

         (Sekolah Kebangsaan Tanah Lalang)

My primary school was Sekolah Kebangsaan Tanah Lalang, a place that holds so many beautiful memories close to my heart. I studied there from 2010 to 2016, and those six years were truly some of the most precious and carefree moments of my life.Looking back, I often find myself longing for those simpler times. Life was all about curiosity, laughter, and endless possibilities.I wish I could go back and relive those moments as a child again, even if only for a day. Sekolah Kebangsaan Tanah Lalang will always hold a special place in my heart. It’s not just a school; it’s a part of who I am—a foundation of cherished memories and valuable lessons that will stay with me forever.













                   High School

     (Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Khir Johari)




I attended Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Khir Johari, located in Sungai Sumun, Perak, from 2017 to 2021. Those years were a defining chapter of my life, filled with both memorable and challenging moments. It was a period where I cultivated friendships, developed essential skills, and faced academic and personal hurdles that shaped my character. Looking back, my high school journey taught me the importance of resilience, adaptability, and the value of working hard toward my goals. These experiences continue to inspire and guide me in my pursuits today.










                    University

     Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) Kedah



I am currently a student at Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) Kedah, at the Sungai Petani campus, where I have been pursuing my studies since 2022. Being part of UiTM has been an enriching journey, offering unique and meaningful experiences that have broadened my perspective. Studying far from home has been both challenging and rewarding, as it has pushed me out of my comfort zone and encouraged me to embrace independence and self-reliance. The environment, vibrant campus life, and diverse community have provided countless opportunities for personal growth and learning. So far, my time here has been filled with unforgettable moments, new friendships, and valuable lessons that I deeply cherish.
